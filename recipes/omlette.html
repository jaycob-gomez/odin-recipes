Veggie Omelette

Description

A delicious and healthy omelette packed with fresh vegetables, perfect for breakfast or a light meal. This recipe is quick to prepare and can be customized with your favorite veggies.

Ingredients

  • 3 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup bell peppers, diced
  • 1/4 cup onion, diced
  • 1/4 cup spinach, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil or butter
  • Grated cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs and milk until well combined. Season with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat olive oil or butter in a non-stick skillet over medium heat.
  3. Add the diced bell peppers and onion to the skillet. Saut√© for about 2-3 minutes until they start to soften.
  4. Add the chopped spinach and cook for another minute until wilted.
  5. Pour the egg mixture into the skillet, ensuring it covers the vegetables evenly.
  6. Cook for about 3-4 minutes, gently lifting the edges with a spatula to allow uncooked egg to flow underneath.
  7. If using cheese, sprinkle it on top of the omelette just before it finishes cooking.
  8. Once the eggs are set but still slightly soft, fold the omelette in half and slide it onto a plate.
  9. Serve hot, optionally garnished with fresh herbs or additional cheese.
